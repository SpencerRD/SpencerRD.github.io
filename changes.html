Parts that Change

Graphics Card

A normal graphics card looks like the image on the left but a laptop graphics card looks like the image on the right or simply doesn’t exist. They have to be flatter and more compact to fit inside the laptop. While it does make them weaker in most scenarios sometimes they can get to be almost as powerful as one in a desktop. Sometimes they don’t get put into a computer but instead be replaced by “on-board graphics”. On-board graphics that the motherboard is the one that supports everything you see. Most motherboards have it but they aren’t not that powerful. With laptops they have to put more effort into making the on-board graphics strong but they still are not that strong in comparison to a desktop.

Motherboard

The motherboard is the brain of the computer along with any electrical device. It has many components on the motherboard like the CPU, a built hard drive, RAM, and a power supply. But the difference between a laptop’s motherboard and a desktop’s motherboard is the backup battery in a desktop motherboard. The point of a backup battery for a desktop is to provide power for componentes that need power. It is rare to find a backup battery in a laptop but it provides power to components that have a power failure but it only provides enough power until it shuts down.
